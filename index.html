Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is WiFionGo?
WiFionGo is a seamless public WiFi access solution from Revolnet Technology. It allows users to connect to WiFi in public places like hotels, apartments, schools, tech parks, and government-managed venues without the hassle of repeatedly entering passwords or logins.
2. How does WiFionGo work?
WiFionGo authenticates users securely using mobile numbers, OTPs, and app-based credentials. Once registered, users can auto-connect to any WiFionGo-enabled hotspot across locations — just like a roaming mobile network.
3. Where can WiFionGo be used?
- Hotels & Restaurants
- Apartments & Housing Societies
- Educational Institutions (Schools, Colleges)
- Tech Parks & Offices
- Airports, Bus Stations, Railway Stations
- Government Offices & Gram Panchayats
